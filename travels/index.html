My Travels



My family and I have visited five countries so far and have gone to many places inside India as a fun trip. All the places had their own fun. We had new experiences and once we came back we shared it with our friends.

Singapore

We visited Singapore in 2013 in the month of March. We saw many places there like the Lego museum, Helix bridge, Santosa Island, Universal Studio, Marina Bay Sand Hotel and Garden By The Bay. They were amazing. One of the best things was the Lego museum. There were people, cars, and other various things made out of Lego blocks. It was a completely new experience.





Australia

We visited Australia in 2015 in the month of March. We went to different places there and saw the Opera House, we did snorkling, my father did sky diving, we saw the Melbourne Cricket Stadium, we saw the Apostles, we did jet skiing, we went by cable car to a rain forest in Brisbane and we also saw the Darling Harbour. It was again a different and an amazing experience to see the vast beauty of Australia.



Dubai

We visited Dubai in 2015 in the month of October. We stayed in Park Avenue and visited many places like the Dubai Mall, The Burj Khalifa, The Atlantis Hotel and the water park inside that, we went for a desert safari and we had a cable car ride. This again was a very unique country and I had a lot of fun.



Thailand/Hong Kong

We visited Thailand and Kong Hong in 2009 in the month of April. We also visited Macau and Bankok. There we experienced something completely different. We saw the Disney Land, we went in caves by boats, we saw an aquarium, Victoria Harbour, we almost did Bunji jumping and we saw James Bond Island. This too was a very memorable experience for me.



Sikkim

We visited Sikkim in 2018 the month of April. There I experienced snow for the frist time. We went to Lachen, Lachung, Nathu la pass, we did paragliding and we visited North Sikkim.
